Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2025

1. What We Collect

  • Account Info: Email, login method (e.g., GitHub)
  • OAuth Configs: Encrypted client IDs, secrets, and tokens
  • API Logs: Metadata about requests (not full payloads)
  • Cookies: For login sessions

2. How We Use Data

To operate the service, manage tokens, monitor abuse, and support users. No advertising or data resale.

3. Data Security

We encrypt sensitive data at rest. Tokens are decrypted only when needed. HTTPS is enforced.

4. Third-Party APIs

We proxy requests to third-party services on your behalf. We do not control those services.

5. Deletion

Request deletion via support@curlmate.dev. Tokens and data are purged within 48 hours.

6. Changes

We'll notify you of major changes to this policy via email or app notification.
